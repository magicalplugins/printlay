Standardising your gang sheet sizes keeps pricing simple and layouts predictable. Here are the dimensions you'll actually use.

## DTF gang sheet sizes

DTF is almost always **roll-fed**, so the **width is fixed** and you choose the length:

- **22" wide** — the industry standard, matching common DTF printers and films.
- **Sold by length** — e.g. 22" × 12", 22" × 24", 22" × 36", or "by the foot".

Selling by length lets customers buy exactly the area they need, and lets you fill the length their order requires.

## UV DTF gang sheet sizes

UV DTF is often **flatbed or smaller roll**, so sizes tend to be fixed tiles:

- **12" × 12"** — a very common UV DTF tile.
- **A3 (11.7" × 16.5")** and **A4 (8.3" × 11.7")** — popular in the UK/EU.

Because UV DTF decals are small, the size of the sheet matters less than how tightly you nest onto it — see the [UV DTF gang sheets guide](/guides/uv-dtf-gang-sheets).

## How to choose

1. **Match your hardware** — never exceed your printable width.
2. **Pick one or two sizes** — don't offer a dozen; it complicates pricing and layout.
3. **Sell DTF by length** — fixed width, variable length is the simplest model.
4. **Leave edge margins** — keep designs a few mm inside the printable edge.
