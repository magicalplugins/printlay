Stop dragging artwork around a canvas at 2am. **Printlay's gang sheet builder** turns a pile of designs into a tight, print-ready sheet in seconds — sized for your printer, spaced for your cutter, and exported ready to print.

## What it does

- **Automatic nesting** — packs your designs tightly, rotating and arranging them to fit the most per sheet.
- **Your sheet sizes** — set to your printer or roll width; DTF by length, UV DTF by tile.
- **Bleed & cut lines** — adds bleed and contour cut lines where you need them.
- **Print-ready export** — a clean PDF with everything your RIP expects.
- **From your catalogue** — fill sheets from your saved designs in a couple of clicks.

## Why it's faster

Building a sheet by hand means sizing a canvas, importing each design, nudging spacing, rotating to fit, checking resolution, drawing cut lines and exporting — every time. Printlay does all of that automatically. What took half an hour takes seconds, and the spacing is consistent every run.

For the background on the technique, see [what is a gang sheet](/guides/what-is-a-gang-sheet) and [how to make a gang sheet](/blog/how-to-make-a-gang-sheet).

## Built for the whole workflow

The builder is part of Printlay's four-move flow: upload or generate a template, program the slot order, fill from your catalogue, and export. It works alongside the [UV DTF gang sheet builder](/features/uv-dtf-gang-sheet-builder) and the [sticker cut line generator](/features/sticker-cut-line-generator) so apparel, hard goods and stickers all run through one tool.
