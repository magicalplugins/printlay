UV DTF lives or dies on **layout** — the decals are small, the volumes are high, and every gap is wasted film. **Printlay's UV DTF gang sheet builder** nests your decals tightly onto AB film, adds kiss-cut contours and bleed, and exports a print-and-cut ready file.

## What it does

- **Tight nesting for small decals** — fills your sheet with mixed sizes, not a wasteful grid.
- **Automatic kiss-cut contours** — a clean cut path around every decal.
- **Bleed built in** — no white edges after application.
- **Your UV DTF sizes** — 12" × 12", A3, A4 or custom up to your bed/roll.
- **Print-and-cut export** — cut lines on a recognised spot colour.

## Why UV DTF needs it most

Because UV DTF decals are small and varied, laying them out by hand is the most tedious gang-sheet job there is — and the easiest to do wastefully. Automating the nesting and the contours means more decals per sheet, lower cost each, and no manual path-drawing.
