**AB film** is the two-part film system used in [UV DTF](/glossary/uv-dtf). The **A film** is the printable layer that carries the UV-cured ink and laminate. The **B film** is the transfer/application layer that lifts the finished design off the A film and presses it onto the product.

The workflow: print and cure on A, laminate, cut, then peel B, apply to the item, burnish, and remove B — leaving the decal bonded to the surface.
