**Bleed** is artwork extended **past the trim or cut line** so that any slight movement during cutting never leaves an unprinted white edge. For stickers and UV DTF, 2–3 mm of bleed beyond the cut is standard.

Because cutters always drift a little, bleed is the safety margin that keeps edges clean. Pair it with a cut path set to overprint. Work out exact sizes with the [bleed & DPI calculator](/tools/bleed-dpi-calculator).
