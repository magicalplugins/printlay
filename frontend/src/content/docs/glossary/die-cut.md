**Die-cut** means cutting through **both the vinyl and the backing**, producing an individual sticker cut to the shape of the design (usually with a small border). It's the classic standalone "die-cut sticker".

It contrasts with [kiss-cut](/glossary/kiss-cut), which leaves the backing intact. Both rely on a [cut contour](/glossary/cut-contour). See [kiss-cut vs die-cut](/blog/kiss-cut-vs-die-cut).
