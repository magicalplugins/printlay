**DTF**, or **Direct to Film**, is a printing method that transfers full-colour designs onto fabric. The design is printed onto PET transfer film, coated with adhesive powder, cured, then heat-pressed onto the garment and peeled.

DTF works on cotton, polyester and blends without screens or weeding, making it ideal for short-run, full-colour apparel. Almost all DTF is printed onto [gang sheets](/glossary/gang-sheet) to keep cost per transfer low.
