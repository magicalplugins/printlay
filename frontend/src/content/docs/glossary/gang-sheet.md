A **gang sheet** is one large printable sheet that holds many separate designs printed together. "Ganging up" combines multiple jobs — or many copies of one design — onto a single sheet so they print and cut in one pass.

The point is cost: you pay for media, ink and printer time roughly per sheet, so filling the sheet spreads that fixed cost across many prints. Gang sheets are standard in DTF, UV DTF and sticker production.
