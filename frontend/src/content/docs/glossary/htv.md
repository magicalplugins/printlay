**HTV** (heat transfer vinyl, also called iron-on or T-shirt vinyl) is a coloured vinyl film that you **cut to shape** with a vinyl cutter, [weed](/glossary/weeding) away the excess, and **heat-press** onto fabric. The colour comes from the vinyl itself, not ink.

It's ideal for **solid-colour** text, numbers, logos and specialty finishes (glitter, flock, holographic). For full-colour or photographic designs, a printed transfer like [DTF](/glossary/dtf) is usually better — see [HTV vs DTF](/blog/htv-vs-dtf). Learn more in the [heat transfer vinyl guide](/guides/heat-transfer-vinyl).
