**Imposition** is the arrangement of artwork on a press sheet to make the best use of material and to suit the printing and finishing process. A [gang sheet](/glossary/gang-sheet) is a kind of imposition that combines many different designs (or copies) onto one sheet.

Good imposition minimises waste and keeps finishing — cutting, folding, trimming — simple and consistent.
