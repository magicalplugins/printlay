**Kiss-cut** means cutting through the **vinyl only**, leaving the paper backing intact. Stickers stay on the sheet and peel off individually — ideal for sticker sheets, multi-packs and easy handling.

It contrasts with [die-cut](/glossary/die-cut), which cuts through both vinyl and backing. Both use the same [cut line](/glossary/cut-line); only the blade depth differs. See [kiss-cut vs die-cut](/blog/kiss-cut-vs-die-cut).
