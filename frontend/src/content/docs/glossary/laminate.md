A **laminate** is a clear protective layer applied over a printed sticker or decal to resist **water, UV fading and scratches**, and to give a matte or gloss finish. It's a standard step in print-and-cut sticker production, applied after printing and before cutting.

Some methods, like [UV DTF](/glossary/uv-dtf), build durability into the transfer itself and may not need a separate laminate. For where lamination fits in production, see [how to start selling custom stickers](/blog/how-to-sell-custom-stickers).
