**Nesting** is fitting designs together as tightly as possible on a sheet to maximise material use. Unlike a rigid grid, true nesting mixes sizes and rotates designs to fill the gaps — fitting far more per sheet.

Tighter nesting directly lowers your cost per print on a [gang sheet](/glossary/gang-sheet). See [how to reduce DTF film waste](/blog/reduce-dtf-film-waste).
