A **spot colour** is a named, pre-mixed colour applied as its own single channel rather than built from [CMYK](/glossary/cmyk). In digital print-and-cut workflows, spot colours also carry **special instructions** — most importantly the **CutContour** spot, which the [RIP](/glossary/rip-software) treats as a [cut path](/glossary/cut-contour) rather than printed ink.

So a spot colour can mean "this exact ink" or "do something special here" — like cut, or apply white — depending on its name and your RIP setup.
