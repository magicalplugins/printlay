**UV DTF** is a transfer method for **hard surfaces** — mugs, bottles, glass, phone cases, signage. Designs are UV-printed and cured onto the **A film**, laminated, then a **B film** lifts and applies them to the product. No heat or water is needed: you peel, position, burnish and remove the transfer film.

Unlike standard [DTF](/glossary/dtf) (which presses onto fabric), UV DTF produces rigid stickers/decals. See the full guide: [UV DTF gang sheets](/guides/uv-dtf-gang-sheets).
