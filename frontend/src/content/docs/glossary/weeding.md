**Weeding** is removing the excess vinyl from around a cut design, leaving only the parts you want to keep or transfer. After the cutter follows the [cut contour](/glossary/cut-contour), you peel away the surrounding material.

Clean weeding depends on the right cut depth and enough gap between designs — too tight and small decals or laminate can tear. It's why [gang sheet](/glossary/gang-sheet) spacing matters, especially for small [UV DTF](/glossary/uv-dtf) decals.
